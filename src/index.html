About me

My programming journey began when I was 22 years old. At that time, I was curious how websites work and started to learn HTML, CSS, Javascript and play with static websites. Later, I discover a diverse ecosystem of Javascript through frameworks like React and Vue. Next, I dive into the server-side programming and encounter with Node.js and Python. I have gained valuable experiences in web development that laid the foundation for the next part of my journey.

I have come to the concept of "hacking" when I watched videos of John Hammond and LiveOverflow. Following their advices, I participate in CTFs like picoCTF, DownUnderCTF, ... and also practice with HackTheBox. To improve web exploitation skill, I use Webgoat as a learning platform. I'm also interested in binary exploitation with many challenges from pwnable.kr and pwnable.tw. There are many things to learn about Cyber Security and this is just the beginning.
